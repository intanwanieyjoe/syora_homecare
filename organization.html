Organization

Rumah Nurul Iman was established at the end of 2000 with the original name of Yayasan Nurul Iman.


HISTORY OF ESTABLISHMENT

Rumah Nurul Iman was founded by Encik Khairul Anuar Bin Mohmad Razi (Former President). Established with the hope of helping underprivileged children such as orphans, the poor, the neglected and the oppressed, by providing shelter and the opportunity to get a perfect education.


In the early stages of its establishment, Rumah Nurul Iman was managed by a committee. The committee has appointed En. Khairul Anuar Bin Mohmad Razi as the Manager/Supervisor of the hostel. He is the person in charge of all matters relating to the operation of the hostel except in matters relating to assets and finances. For some reason in 2004 the operation moved to Jalan Intan, Kampung Dato Ahmad Said.


At the end of 2005 again these children were moved, this time due to the failure of the Nurul Iman Foundation to fund the operating costs of the hostel and problems with the rented landlord. As a result of facing problems too often, then Mr. Khairul Anuar is determined to take over the full operation of the Nurul Iman Hostel from the Nurul Iman Foundation and its Committee.


This new settlement is located at Plot 359, Lorong Dato, Manjoi, Ipoh, Perak. The settlement only has a two -door rental house that is rented at a rate of RM 200.00 per month. With a meager personal income, En. Khairul Anuar had worked hard to operate the settlement with the help of the surrounding residents and the then Manjoi ADUN, YB Dato 'Haji Nazri. After trying hard to defend the fate of these orphans and poor children alone, including cooking and everything, then on October 9, 2006 Mr. Khairul Anuar has officially registered the Nurul Iman Orphans and Poor Child Care Center as a sole proprietorship. On the 12th of October, Mr. Khairul Anuar has made a sworn statement to confirm that the Nurul Iman Orphans and Poor Child Care Center conducts activities for free.


To expand the protection of orphans and the poor, In June 2010 En. Khairul Anuar has opened another Care Center (Branch) in Kampung Pinang, Kamunting, Taiping which accommodates only boys but was closed on 1st September 2016 due to lack of support.


And on 30 June 2011 PERTUBUHAN KEBAJIKAN KASIH NURUL IMAN NEGERI PERAK was officially established under the Registrar of Societies. This establishment is to streamline the administration and fulfill the requirements of the Social Welfare Department.
